Contacts

The Panther Station: General Guidance

The Panther Station can offer advisement on many different domains, including admissions and financial aid. If they are unable to assist in a matter, they can guide you towards the right department or resource. They are highly reachable through many forms of communication, which makes them a good first stop. All of their contact information, as well as their hours of operation, can be found on their web page. Click here to visit the Panther Station web page.

Academic Advisement

The Academic Advisement & Transfer Center (AATC) can be contacted about any questions or concerns you may have about opting for or receiving a grade. You can do this through an Academic Advisor. If you do not have one assigned, or do not know how to contact them, you can reach the AATC at aatc@bmcc.cuny.edu. All of their contact information, as well as their hours of operation, can be found on their web page. Click here to visit the Academic Advisement web page.

Financial Aid

The Financial Aid department can be contacted about any questions or concerns you may have about your financial aid status or eligibility. It's important to keep in mind that receiving grades such as "W" and "NC" can impact your financial aid eligibility, and it is wise to discuss this with a representative. The Financial Aid department's contact information, as well as their hours of operation, can be found on their web page. Click here to visit the Financial Aid department web page.

Counseling Center

If you, or anyone you know, is struggling with any emotional or adjustment issues, or any other problems that are impacting your academic performance, you are invited to attend FREE and CONFIDENTIAL sessions with a school counselor. Counselors can help you work through your difficulties and plan ahead for what is to come. All of the Counseling Center's contact information, as well as their hours of operation, can be found on their web page. Click here to visit the Counseling Center web page.
