"FIN" Grade

You Should Know

The "FIN" (Failure From Incomplete) grade is assigned when a student fails to meet the deadline for an "INC" grade. The "INC" deadline can be found on the BMCC Academic Calendar. All missing coursework must be completed no later than the deadline posted on the Academic Calendar. If your work is not submitted by that deadline, your "INC" grade will become a "FIN" grade. A "FIN" grade factors into your GPA as an "F" (Failure) grade, and faculty may not be able to change it into another letter grade after the deadline. Due to the fact that "FIN" grades are treated as "F" grades, they will negatively affect GPA, and can impact financial aid status.

"FIN" grades are avoidable. If you have obtained an "INC" grade, make sure to complete all work assigned by your instructor by the appropriate deadlines in order to earn your standard letter grade.

Get In Touch

If you are a recipient of a "FIN" grade, or are expecting to get one, and have any questions or concerns, contact StudentSuccess@bmcc.cuny.edu. If you are a member of ASAP, BLA, College Discovery, or CUNY EDGE, you may choose to contact your advisor. If you would like to get in a touch with a relevant department, you may refer to the Contacts page for more information.
